Zoomorphic Vase

Ceramic Sculpture • 2024

Dog-shaped vessel in barro rojo, hand-shaped using traditional Zapotec techniques. Burnished with quartz and unglazed, inspired by pre-Hispanic ceramic forms. Created in Oaxaca, Mexico.

Botanical Mural

Wall Painting • 2024

Large-scale mural in Oxacan hostel celebrating organic forms and tropical flora, bringing warmth and life to community spaces through bold, earthy tones.

Classical Study

Graphite Drawing • 2024

Study of the Death of Senecea by Manuel Dominguez Sanchez in the Museo Nacional del Prado, exploring human form and gesture.

About
